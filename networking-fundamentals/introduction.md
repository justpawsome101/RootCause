# Introduction

Surprisingly enough this is the topic I struggled with the most. This was mainly due to me underestimating the complexity of networking. Networking also happens to be the most important concept fundamentally. So please don't be like me and be like "oh wow its just networks I know that computers talk to other computers its not that deep, whatever lets skip to the interesting stuff." Trust me it will come back to bite you.😭

"Before you can break a network, you need to understand how it works."

Everything in cybersecurity theoretically builds of this section so **LOCK IN**. And yes I know it can be a drag but if I could get through writing this than you can get through reading it.

This chapter starts from absolute zero and builds up to offensive technique. Every concept is framed through an attacker's lens. It's not just _what_ protocols do, but _why_ they fail and _how_ they're exploited.&#x20;
